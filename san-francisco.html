SAN FRANCISCO / BAY AREA, CALIFORNIA

The Bay Area is good to visit all year round. You can walk around the neighborhoods in San Francisco, visit some beaches in the south bay, or even go up to Napa Vally for wine tasting. You will find that the Bay Area offers more than just giant tech companies. Tourist arrractions in San Francisco are always packed. I picked the following three activities so that you could avoid the crowd and enjoy the beautiful city.

  • San Francisco Museum of Modern Art - SFMOMA is a must go if you like art. You can find a wide variety of art from all around the world. It's probably one of the top art museums in the nation.
  • Golden Gate Park - It is a public park which extends from Stanyan Street to the Pacific Ocean. You can find locals jogging, walking dogs, and getting together to play music. Also, check out the botanical garden if you have enough time.
  • Wine tasting in Napa Valley - Napa Vally is well known for it's vineyards and wineries. Making a trip from San Francisco only takes about an hour. Book in advance if you want a private tasting. Check out this newly open winery Ashes & Dimonds, very stylish and comfortable space for you to enjoy your time there.
